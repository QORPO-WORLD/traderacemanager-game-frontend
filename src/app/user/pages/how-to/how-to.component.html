Intro

GAMEPLAN

Trade Race Manager (TRM) is a completely free-to-play model with the possibility to maximize your profits with NFTs.


The new game system is based on every-day rewards up to 1 500 IOI. Become part of IOI Army and play TRM to enjoy unlimited rewards.


Guaranteed prize pools up to 10 IOI in NFT races take place every 10 minutes and special every-day NFT tournament with a guaranteed prize pool of 100 IOI for NFT holders.


Free Fast races take place every 2 minutes for everybody with a guaranteed prize pool of 0.28 IOI.


Free members can race with NON-NFT car but receive only 0.1 IOI per win in TOP 3 spots.

Gameplan logic

NFT CARS

Your winnings are based on the NFT car tier you own. There are several NFT car tiers ranging from common to legendary.


There is also a special RARE NFT car in each tier that is received for FREE when you own every car from the specific tier! RARE NFT cars are adding multiplier to possible IOI win from every race.


A race takes place every 10 minutes and is completely free to play - every race is happening on a different track.


Races are free for NFT car holders as well as for NON NFT cars holders. However NON NFT car holders can win maximum of 0.1 IOI for any of top 3 positions in the race.

Seasons &

Ranking

What you need to start the season is the Premium membership.


Sooner you get the premium membership, the sooner you start getting points to the ranking system for sharing IOI prize pool at the end of the season.


The season takes 1 month (start is always on the first day of the month) and resets on the last day of the month.


The ranking system is simple. You receive points (check the point chart below) for every race you win as the premium member. At the end of the season (month), the first 10 Leaderboard racers are splitting the prize pool of 1 000 IOI based on their results by the end of the season.


The premium members are playing for 3 000 IOI every month — 2 000 IOI prize pool with their teams (TOP 3 teams) and 1 000 IOI prize pool for the overall best racers (TOP 10 racers).
