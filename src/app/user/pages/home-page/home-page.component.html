Staking rewards for your cars

Staking is a feature that allows owners of traderacemanager Collectibles to earn rewards from them in the form of IOI.

If you own a car Collectible, you can Stake this and for the period that it is Staked, you will earn IOI. The amount of IOI earned will be dependent on the total amount of cars Staked, the Rarity of the car, and the size of the reward pool.

While Staked, the owner cannot sell or play with the car. That is the tradeoff for earning from it. But they are able to remove it from Staking at any time.
