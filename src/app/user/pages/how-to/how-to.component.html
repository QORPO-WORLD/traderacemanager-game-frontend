NFT CARS

Get NFT car and race for big rewards forever!

Trade Race Manager (TRM) is completely free to play model with possibility to maximize your profits with NFTs. Your winning are based on the NFT car tier you own. There are several NFT car tiers ranging from common to legendary. In each tier there is special RARE NFT car that is received when you own every car from the tier for FREE! RARE NFT cars are adding multiplier to possible IOI win from every race.

Race takes place every 10 minutes and is completely free to play.Each race in 1 hour happening on a different track.

Races are free for NFT cars forever as well as for NON NFT cars but with the max win of 0,1 IOI for any of top 3 positions for NON NFT Car.

Ranking

Get premium and your place on the leaderboard!

Each month premium members are playing for 2000 IOI with their teams (TOP 3 teams) and 1000 IOI for the overall best racers (TOP 10 racers) - 3000 IOI Every month!

Season takes 1 month (start is always the first day of the month) and resets at the last day of the month

You need Premium membership to get points for the season. Sooner you will get premium sooner you are legible for ranking system and IOI prize pool at the end of the season

With every race you win as premium member you receive points (check the point chart). By the end of the month first 10 racers form the leaderboard are legible for the prize pool of 1000 IOI based on their results throughout the season.
