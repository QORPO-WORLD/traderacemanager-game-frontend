Seasons and Ranking

What you need to start the season is the Premium membership.

Sooner you get the premium membership, the sooner you start getting points to the ranking system for sharing IOI prize pool at the end of the season.

The season takes 1 month (start is always on the first day of the month) and resets on the last day of the month.

The ranking system is simple. You receive points (check the point chart below) for every race you win as the premium member. At the end of the season (month), the first 10 Leaderboard racers are splitting the prize pool of 1 000 IOI based on their results by the end of the season.

The premium members are playing for 3 000 IOI every month — 2 000 IOI prize pool with their teams (TOP 3 teams) and 1 000 IOI prize pool for the overall best racers (TOP 10 racers).
