How the Tournament race system works

  • The race has 19 total rounds.
  • It is divided into 2 types. Round where you collect points and elimination rounds.
  • In the first 10 races, everyone needs to collect points to secure 10th position or above.
  • Elimination rounds will be active after the first 10 races and all players below 10th position will be eliminated instantly.
  • In upcoming rounds there will be always one player - placed last - eliminated. After 10 races there will be only one last remaining player declared the winner.
